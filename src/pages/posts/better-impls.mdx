## Inspiration

I was going through my RSS feed aggregator on a lazy Sunday when I encountered [an interesting 2021 blog post](https://cbarrete.com/rust-bad.html) about the author's dislikes of Rust. Even in 2025, I agree with all of the points they made in the post. The one issue that really stook out to me though, were the complaints about `impl` blocks in Rust.
